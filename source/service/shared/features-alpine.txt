Features
========

* Deployed in 3 stages (:ref:`install <one_service_stage_install>`, :ref:`configure <one_service_stage_configure>`, :ref:`bootstrap <one_service_stage_bootstrap>`). More general :ref:`information <one_service_appliance>`.
* Based on the `Alpine x86-64 Linux distribution <https://alpinelinux.org>`_.
* No default login (local or SSH) password - must be provided via `contextualization <http://docs.opennebula.org/stable/operation/vm_setup/kvm.html#user-credentials>`_.
